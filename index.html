🎯 Our Mission

KL has endless options but no time to sift through them. JomOutKL cuts the noise. Every day we read the weather, traffic, events, and prices — then tell you the smart move, whether you're a local deciding where to makan, a tourist figuring out the city, or friends catching up and need somewhere good, fast.

📖 The Story

Built by a KL local tired of group chats asking "where to makan?" every single day. JomOutKL is the answer that never sleeps — a daily guide that knows the city's rhythm, from mamak runs to hidden cafés to weekend escapes up to Cameron or down to Melaka.

📖 KL Lingo

A few local words you will see around JomOutKL and all over KL:

Jom — Let us go! / Come on! The friendly nudge to get out the door.
Makan — To eat, or food in general. “Let us makan” = let us grab a bite.
Mamak — A 24-hour Indian-Muslim eatery. Late-night roti, teh tarik, the works.
Teh tarik — “Pulled tea”, sweet milky tea poured back and forth for froth.
Pasar malam — Night market. Pop-up street stalls, usually one fixed night a week.
Lepak — To chill / hang out with no rush. The KL art of relaxing.
Cabut — To take off / leave. “Cabut jap” = pop out for a bit.
Panas — Hot. As in the KL afternoon sun. Very panas.
